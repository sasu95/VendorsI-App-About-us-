About Us

Vendorsiapp.com Established in 2024, VendorsI is an Egyptian e-commerce and retail company

Through our mobile application, we connect honest sellers with honest buyers.

Vision

Empowering youth vendors to thrive through organized and simplified selling experiences. Our app is dedicated to fostering community support; by providing a seamless platform for emerging vendors to showcase their products. For our clients, we are committed to curating a one-stop shop for useful products. With our guarantees and customer-friendly refund policies, we aim to redefine convenience, and ensure a hassle-free shopping experience for everyone.

Let’s Be Partners

One of VendorsI’s objectives is to provide new opportunities and empower new vendors. To make transactions better and more frictionless, we developed unique technology. If you are a vendor, delivery person, or business, we would love to have you as a partner on our journey. Get in touch with us now.
